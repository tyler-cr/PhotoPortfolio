biography

Tyler Crimando is an Arizona based photographer with work featured in art galleries spanning from Los Angeles, New York, and Rome. His work is eclectic in structure, with emphasis on the male form, alternative architecture, and street photography.

He was born 1999 in Phoenix, Arizona. He learned photography at The University of Arizona, and graduated in 2022.
